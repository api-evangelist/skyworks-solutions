# Skyworks Solutions

Skyworks Solutions, Inc. is an American semiconductor company headquartered in Irvine, California that designs, develops, and markets proprietary semiconductor products for wireless communications, automotive, broadband, connected home, industrial, defense, and consumer electronics applications. Skyworks is a leading provider of analog and mixed-signal semiconductors with approximately 35% market share in 5G RF front-end modules.

## Products

Skyworks' product portfolio spans a wide range of analog and mixed-signal semiconductor components:

- **Front-End Modules** — Integrated RF transmit/receive modules for 5G, LTE, Wi-Fi
- **Power Amplifiers** — High-efficiency RF amplification for mobile and infrastructure
- **Filters** — Band-pass filters for multi-band wireless systems
- **Switches** — RF switching for antenna and band selection
- **Digital Isolators** — High-speed isolation with integrated power (Si86Px family)
- **Low-Noise Amplifiers** — Receive path amplification for wireless systems
- **Attenuators, Mixers, Oscillators, PLLs** — Signal chain components
